Why Bitcoin?

We accept Bitcoin for several reasons aligned with our project values:

  • Censorship Resistance — No intermediary can block contributions to controversial research
  • Global Access — Contributors anywhere in the world can participate
  • Transparency — All transactions are publicly verifiable on the blockchain
  • Philosophical Alignment — Decentralized systems mirror our governance principles

Want to contribute via other means? Contact us to discuss alternatives.

Transparency Commitment

We believe donors deserve to know exactly how their contributions are used.

  • Quarterly public reports on all income and expenditures
  • Bitcoin address balance publicly verifiable on blockchain
  • Major expenditures documented with receipts
  • No funds used for anything unrelated to project mission
  • Clear separation between funding and technical decisions

Donor Recognition

We're happy to recognize donors who wish to be acknowledged. Options include:

  • Public Recognition — Listed on our supporters page with contribution tier
  • Anonymous — Contribution noted but no name published
  • Pseudonymous — Listed under a chosen name or handle
  • Private — No public acknowledgment at all

After donating, contact us with your transaction ID and preferred recognition option. If we don't hear from you, contributions remain anonymous by default.

Important Notes

  • Mir.ai Technology is not currently a registered nonprofit. Donations may not be tax-deductible depending on your jurisdiction.
  • Bitcoin transactions are irreversible. Please double-check the address before sending.
  • For large contributions, consider reaching out first to discuss impact and recognition.
